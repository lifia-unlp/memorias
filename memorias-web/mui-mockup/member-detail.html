John Smith is a Full Professor at the School of Computer Science, National University of La Plata (UNLP), and a Principal Researcher of CONICET at LIFIA. He received his PhD in Computer Science in 2010. His research interests focus on distributed systems, semantic web, and agile software development methodologies.

Education & Academic Background:

  • PhD in Computer Science - National University of La Plata (2010)
  • B.S. in Computer Science - National University of La Plata (2005)
  • Full Professor - Software Architecture Area, School of Computer Science, UNLP (2015 — Present)
  • Principal Researcher - National Scientific and Technical Research Council (CONICET) (2020 — Present)

Mis intereses actuales incluyen la interoperabilidad semántica en sistemas distribuidos de IoT, diseño e implementación de arquitecturas basadas en microservicios, y la incorporación de tecnologías de registros distribuidos en plataformas académicas.

My current research interests include semantic interoperability in distributed IoT systems, design and implementation of microservice architectures, and the incorporation of distributed ledger technologies in academic portals.
